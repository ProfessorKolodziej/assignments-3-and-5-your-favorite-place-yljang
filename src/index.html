Tips for South Korea

  • Respect the elders.
  • South Korea is a very safe country.
  • There is Wi-Fi available everywhere.
  • Public transportation system is very good. Always take subway or buses.
  • Tipping isn't necessary. It's not part of Korean culture, and it could come off as rude.
